본문콘텐츠영역

  • Evil Has Survived

    The Archangel Tyrael is presumed dead, and mankind is left to deal with the aftermath of his actions. Fragments of the shattered Worldstone, still embued with great power, are tainting the land. Diablo’s minions hope to harness that power to allow the Lord of Terror to return.

AN UNTOLD CHAPTER IN THE
DIABLO SAGA

The three great demons were defeated, but the world stone separating heaven, hell, and sanctuary was contaminated and destroyed by Tyrell's hands. Twenty years after the world stone was destroyed and the boundaries disappeared, a comet suddenly appeared in the night sky of the sanctuary world and fell into the tristrum, and the sanctuary world was engulfed in an ominous energy.

NEW HORRORS ARISE

Strange creatures and ancient apparitions come to life in Diablo III. Beware what lurks in the shadows.

  • THE SKELETON KING

    The Mad King Leoric was driven to madness by Diablo, Lord of Terror. The horrors he committed in life would be rivaled only by those in his death should his restless spirit and his undead army return to Sanctuary…

  • Haunted Carriage

    Adventurers traveling through Ashwold Cemetery are sure to become well-acquainted with the haunted carriage, which is said to carry the souls of the dead.

  • COUNTESS

    The Countess was raised in an isolated, forested castle in Khanduras. As she grew older, she bathed in the blood of hundreds of captives to retain her ebbing youth. When her wickedness was ended, she was interred in the castle's tower, for she could not be truly killed.

  • BAAL

    One of the three Prime Evils and corruptor of the Worldstone, Baal revels in absolute annihilation whether he incites or inspires it. The Lord of Destruction would have razed all humanity from Sanctuary if not for the Archangel Tyrael’s sacrifice.
